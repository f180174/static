Bill Gates Books

Gates has written three books:

  1. The Road Ahead , written with Microsoft executive Nathan Myhrvold and journalist Peter Rinearson , was published in November 1995. It summarized the implications of the personal computing revolution and described a future profoundly changed by the arrival of a global information superhighway.

  2. Business @ the Speed of Thought was published in 1999, and discusses how business and technology are integrated, and shows how digital infrastructures and information networks can help to get an edge on the competition.

  3. How to Avoid a Climate Disaster , to be published in 2021, Gates shares what he has learned in over a decade of studying climate change and investing in innovations to address climate problems.
